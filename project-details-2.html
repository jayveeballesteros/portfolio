PropNex PA Malaysia

PropNex PA Malaysia is a notable project in my portfolio. This application is designed to enhance the efficiency of real estate professionals in Malaysia.

I was responsible for porting the Chope module that I developed from the PN Business Suite to PropNex PA Malaysia, ensuring its advanced features were seamlessly integrated.


Chope module includes:

  • Smart scheduling tools for effortless posting of listings in portals.
  • Joint listing capabilities across multiple platforms.
  • Enhanced lead management to engage prospects effectively.

PropNex PA Malaysia highlights my ability to adapt and implement existing solutions into new environments, enhancing their functionality and user experience.
