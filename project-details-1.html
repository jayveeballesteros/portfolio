PN Business Suite

As part of my journey as an iOS developer, PN Business Suite holds a special place as one of my most impactful projects. Designed with the real estate professional in mind, this platform embodies innovation and efficiency, reshaping how agents and teams manage their workflows.

I worked on the ProWeb and Chope modules, bringing cutting-edge solutions to life.

ProWeb features:

  • Hassle-free domain purchasing and subdomain integration.
  • Website generation with dynamic header/footer customization.
  • Website content editing with AI, page sorting and SEO and script settings

Additionally, the Chope Module empowers agents with:

  • Smart scheduling tools for effortless posting of listings in portals.
  • Joint listing capabilities across multiple platforms.
  • Enhanced lead management to engage prospects effectively.

PN Business Suite is a testament to my passion for creating solutions that empower users and drive innovation.
